I'm

Pinoy, IT Enthusiast

I am a Filipino based in Wellington who is deeply passionate about the world of Information Technology and Automation. My fervor for this field has driven me to continuously learn and explore new developments in the industry, allowing me to stay at the forefront of the latest technological advancements.

This website serves as a platform for me to showcase my academic endeavors during my tenure at Otago Polytechnic in the field of Information Technology. It has been designed using BootstrapMade, a white background, and fonts. The selection of this particular design was driven by its minimalistic aesthetic, which conveys a sense of simplicity and organization. In addition to my academic accomplishments, I am currently employed as a System Administrator, leveraging my expertise in Information Technology to provide comprehensive system solutions. Prior to my current role, I served as an IT Support Analyst, where I gained practical experience in troubleshooting and providing technical assistance to clients. These experiences have further refined my skills and provided me with a holistic understanding of the field, enabling me to excel in my current position.
